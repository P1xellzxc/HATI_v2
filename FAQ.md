# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

### What is HATI²?
HATI² is a personal finance management application that helps you track your expenses and manage your budget efficiently.

### Is it free to use?
Yes, the core application is free to use.

### Does it work offline?
Yes! HATI² is built with an "offline-first" approach. You can add expenses, view your dashboard, and perform most actions without an internet connection. Your data will automatically sync with the server once you are back online.

## Privacy & Data

### Is my data safe?
We take data security seriously. Your data is stored locally on your device and securely synced to our servers using industry-standard encryption. Authentication is handled via Supabase Auth.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't login, what should I do?
*   Check your internet connection.
*   Ensure you are using the correct email and password.
*   If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

### The app crashes on startup.
*   Ensure your Android OS version is supported (Android 8.0 / API 26 and above).
*   Clear the app cache/data in your phone settings and try again.

### Why isn't my data syncing?
Syncing requires an active internet connection. Check if you are connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data. If you are online and it still won't sync, the server might be experiencing temporary downtime.
